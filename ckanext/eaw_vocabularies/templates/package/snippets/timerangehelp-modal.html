Examples

Use only Time-Start for year, month, day, hour, minute, second:

Time-StartTime-End
2015any time in 2015
2015-03any time in March 2015
2015-03-11any time on March 11, 2015
2015-03-11T23March 3, 2015, [23:00 .. 24:00)
1969-10-29T22:30:04.765a particular millisecond

Use Time-Start and Time-End for explicit time-ranges:
Time-StartTime-End
20142016any time in 2014, 2015 or 2016
1969-061969-08any time in the summer 1969
1969-07-16T13:32:001969-07-24T16:50:35during the flight of Apollo 11
-9700*in the Holocene
*-0001Before Common Era
